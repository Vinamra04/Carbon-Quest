Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Carbon Emissions Tracker work?

The tracker calculates your carbon footprint based on your daily activities, such as transportation, energy usage, and waste. It provides insights and recommendations to help you reduce your emissions.

Is the tracker free to use?

Yes, the Carbon Emissions Tracker is completely free to use. We believe that everyone should have access to tools that help them live more sustainably.

How accurate are the emissions calculations?

Our calculations are based on the latest data and research. While they provide a good estimate, individual results may vary based on specific circumstances.
